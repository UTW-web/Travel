Welcome to My Travel Blog

About Me

I don’t collect countries. I collect feelings. Moments that smell like woodsmoke and pine. Afternoons that taste like ripe tomatoes picked straight from the garden. Mornings wrapped in mountain silence. Evenings softened by candlelight, the sound of geese and the quiet paws of cats under the stars.

My name is Maria. I live close to the earth – between forests, a garden, and the animals who keep me company. I don’t travel to escape life, but to meet it more fully. To feel. To remember. To return home changed.

Here, I share pieces of that journey. Not just the places – but how they breathe. Not just what I see – but how it moves me. Maybe you’ll recognize something in it too.
