Welcome to My Travel Blog

About Me

This is my path. A path through lands, flavors, experiences, and feelings… At heart, I’m a nomad, and my soul’s journey is one of exploration, tasting, creating, sensing, and connecting. If you're looking for classic travel blogs, you won’t find them here. But you will find many sincere reflections and feelings from my journey. My name is Maria. For a long time, I believed that the meaning of life was in work, parenting, and everyday survival—but deep inside, I felt there was something more calling me. And that’s how love for myself was born— alongside the exploration of my own depths.

Today I live a simple, slow life in the countryside— alongside the love of my life, two curious geese, and two gentle cats. I feel connected to myself, to nature, and to the universe. My motto? To be here and now. Fully present in this moment. I start my mornings with a glass of warm water with lemon, watching nature unfold. These are the moments when life feels enough. When I feel peace and freedom. From this silence and presence, this website was born.

If something in my words resonates with your own journey, I am grateful. Grateful that I can offer a small piece of my path to a world longing for more authenticity. Each of us carries something unique. I simply offer a glimpse into mine.

My Sunrises & Sunsets

My Sunrise

Each sunrise is a new beginning. Not just of the day, but of me. When the sky gently glows, I don’t just watch it—I breathe it in. With every inhale, I fill my body with light. I awaken my cells, greet my organs, and whisper: “Good morning. I am here. I am alive.” I collect these moments as reminders that everything can start anew. That each day is a chance to shine—from within. These are my sunrises. May they touch yours too.

The most beautiful sunset is at home.

I’ve seen many of them. Those fiery ones over the desert, softly pink over the ocean, dramatic in the mountains and quiet above city rooftops. Each one caught me for a moment and reminded me how beautiful it is when the day respectfully bows into the night.But the most beautiful sunset? It’s on the terrace of my little house. There, where I know every corner of the light. Where the wind brings familiar scents. Where the sky paints a different farewell each evening, but the feeling remains the same: this is me, I belong here. Nothing is more calming, nothing more real. Maybe because nothing disturbs me. Because there’s nothing to catch, prove, or capture. I just sit. I just am. And the world goes to sleep.
